The Natural Earth Administrative Boundaries provided in MISLAND-Africa are in the public domain. The boundaries and names used, and the designations used, in MISLAND-Africa do not imply official endorsement or acceptance by Conservation International Foundation, or by its partner organizations and contributors.

If using MISLAND-Africa for official purposes, it is recommended that users choose an official boundary provided by the designated office of their country.
